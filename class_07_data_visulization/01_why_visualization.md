# Why Data Visualization?

## Definition (Simple English)

Data visualization converts numbers into pictures (charts) so people can understand patterns quickly.

## Why do we use it?

- To find trends, patterns and outliers quickly.
- To communicate results to others clearly.

## Real-world Example

A shop's monthly sales numbers are hard to read in a table; a line chart shows the trend at a glance.
